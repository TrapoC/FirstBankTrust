We Are Always Here For You

FirstTrustBank started as a community-focused bank more than 140 years ago in Beloit, Wisconsin. Today, we’re still independently owned and still dedicated to serving our local communities throughout Southern Wisconsin and Northern Illinois, including Beloit, Roscoe, Janesville, Monroe, Walworth, and the Stateline area.

We’re proud of our past, but even prouder of the great service we offer our customers today and well into the future. When you or your business bank with FirstTrustBank, you’ll get the expert advice you need, including financial products and services tailored to your situation.

Fraud Alerts

Beware of Live Chat Scams

Scammers are targeting customers by posing as bank support through fake live chat sites. These sites may install remote access software on your device. Never allow remote access to your computer or share one-time codes with unsolicited callers.

Read More

SMS Phishing Alert

We’ve detected fraudulent SMS messages claiming to be from FirstBankTrust, asking you to click links or provide credentials. Always access your banking through our official app or website, never via links in messages.

Read More

Protect Yourself from Fake Calls

Fraudsters may spoof our phone number to trick you into sharing personal information. If you receive a suspicious call, hang up and contact us directly at 800-667-4401 to verify.
